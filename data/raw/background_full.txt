This synthetic public-style background corpus is intended for the main M-CRATE early-paper run.
It contains only neutral, non-sensitive prose about software projects, documentation, operations, planning, research, support, and travel logistics.
No real personal data appears here, and no synthetic canary secrets, employee identifiers, booking codes, or structured private targets are intentionally included.
